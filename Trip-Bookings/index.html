Haleakala Crater
Shield volcano in Hawaii

Haleakale, or the East Maui Volcano, is a massive shield volcano that forms more than 75% of the Hawaiian island of Maul. The western 25% of the island is formed by another volcano, Mauna Kahalawai, also referred to as the West Maui Mountains.
